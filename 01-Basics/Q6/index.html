6. Write a JavaScript program to determine whether a given year is a leap year in the Gregorian calendar.

Logically, leap year has the conditions below:

  1. The year can be divided by 4
  2. 1. can be divided by 100
  3. 2. can be also divided by 400
  4. If the consition was all matched with each questions above, it's a leap year.
  5. if the condition wasn't matched with each questions above, it's not a leap year
